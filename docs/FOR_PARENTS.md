# KickrIQ for Parents
*A plain-English guide to what your athlete is using and why it's safe.*

---

## What is KickrIQ?

KickrIQ is an AI app that helps your son or daughter get recruited to play college soccer. It's free.

It does five things for them, automatically:

1. **Builds their recruiting profile** so coaches see the right info up front.
2. **Finds the right colleges** by comparing their stats and grades against 771 real college soccer programs.
3. **Writes emails to coaches** that sound like your athlete, not like spam.
4. **Rates their highlight video** the way a college coach would, with specific feedback on what to fix.
5. **Tracks every coach reply** so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Is it safe for my child?

Yes. Here is exactly what we do:

- **Under 13:** We do not let anyone under 13 sign up. Period. This is required by federal law (COPPA) and we enforce it at the signup screen.
- **Ages 13 to 17:** We collect a parent email and require explicit parental consent before the account becomes active. You get notified.
- **Data:** We never sell athlete data. Ever. Full data list is in our [Privacy Policy](https://kickriq.com/privacy).
- **Email sending:** When the app sends a coach email, it sends from your athlete's own Gmail account, with their permission, after they review the draft. Nothing goes out automatically without their click.
- **Account deletion:** You can request account and data deletion any time by emailing infokickriq@gmail.com.

## What does my athlete actually do with it?

A typical recruiting workflow on KickrIQ:

1. **Day 1:** They fill out their profile. Position, club, GPA, stats, highlight video, division goal (D1, D2, D3, NAIA, JUCO). Takes about 15 minutes.
2. **Day 1:** They get a list of college programs that actually fit them, ranked by athletic match and academic match. Reach schools, target schools, safety schools.
3. **Week 1:** They paste their highlight video. The AI rates it and tells them exactly what to cut, what to add, and what's holding it back.
4. **Week 1:** They start drafting coach emails. The AI writes the draft, they edit, they send from their own Gmail.
5. **Ongoing:** When coaches reply, the app catches it, rates the temperature of the response (interested, lukewarm, or polite no), and tells your athlete what to send next.

You get to see all of it, if you want.

## Common parent questions

**"Will college coaches actually read these emails?"**
Yes. The whole point of writing them with AI is that they sound like a real teenager, not a service. They go from your athlete's real Gmail. They include the things coaches actually care about: graduation year, position, club, stats, GPA, and a clear ask.

**"Is the AI going to write something embarrassing?"**
Every email is reviewed by your athlete before sending. The AI drafts. The athlete edits. The athlete sends.

**"What if my child doesn't want me involved?"**
That's their call, but we encourage families to do this together. Recruiting decisions are family decisions.

**"What data do you share with the AI?"**
Only what's needed to draft the email or rate the video: profile fields, school information, highlight URL. We don't share your athlete's contact list, social media, or anything else.

**"Who built this?"**
Two founders, Nico and Kova. No outside investors. No corporate parent. We built KickrIQ because the existing options charge thousands of dollars and don't actually work that well.
