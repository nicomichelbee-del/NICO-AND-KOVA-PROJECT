The smartest way to get recruited.

KickrIQ is an AI college soccer recruitment counselor that does the work athletes used to pay $1,200 to $6,000 a year for. It builds the recruiting profile, matches across 771 college programs, drafts coach emails that get replies, rates highlight videos with computer vision, and tracks the entire pipeline from first contact to commitment. Two founders. No outside capital. ~$50 a month to operate. Free for every user at launch, with paid tiers held behind a waitlist.
