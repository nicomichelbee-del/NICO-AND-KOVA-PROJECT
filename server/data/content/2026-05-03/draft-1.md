# Draft 1 — The recruiting rejection spiral: athlete sends 30 emails, gets 2 responses, doesn't know why — until they see what D1 coaches actually want in a profile.

**Format:** both · **Audience:** athlete

## Hook (0-1.5s)
> You sent 30 recruiting emails and got ghosted by 28 coaches. Here's why.

## Suggested Sound
- **POV text-on-screen trend (chill lo-fi beat)** (`pov-text-trend`)
- Vibe: relatable, conversational, text-heavy
- Example to search in app: rotating lo-fi instrumentals — pick the top trending one this week
- Why: Athlete pain point (ghosting) is the core of the hook, needs conversational, text-heavy vibe that feels like a peer dropping truth.

## Script
- **0-1.5s** — Close-up of phone screen showing Gmail inbox with red 'no response' notifications, athlete's face looking defeated
  - VO: "You sent 30 recruiting emails and got ghosted by 28 coaches."
- **1.5-4s** — Split-screen: left side shows generic 'Dear Coach' email template, right side shows phone with KickrIo profile builder open, athlete's stats and highlights properly formatted
  - VO: "It's not that you're not good enough. Your profile is invisible. Coaches need to see your GPA, your speed numbers, your position flexibility, your highlight reel — all in one place they can actually scan in 10 seconds."
- **4-7s** — Screen recording of KickrIo profile builder: athlete fills in height, weight, club team, GPA, best clips. Profile auto-generates a clean card.
  - VO: "Build your recruiting profile once. Then every coach sees what they're actually looking for."
- **7-10s** — Phone notification sound + coach response email appears on screen, athlete smiles
  - VO: "The difference between ghosted and getting official visit offers? A profile coaches can actually read."
- **10-12s** — KickrIo app icon on phone home screen, athlete tapping it

## On-screen Text
- **0-2s** [top] "28 GHOSTED"
- **2-5s** [middle] "Generic emails = invisible"
- **5-8s** [middle] "Smart profile = visible"
- **8-12s** [bottom] "Link in bio to build yours free"
